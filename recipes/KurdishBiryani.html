Kurdish Biryani Recipe

Describtion

Kurdish biryani is a vibrant rice dish that tantalizes the taste buds with its unique combination of savory spices, tender protein, and a delightful surprise of sweet and crunchy elements . Unlike the layered Indian version, Kurdish biryani features fluffy rice, typically Basmati, seasoned with warm spices like turmeric. This fragrant base becomes the canvas for an array of colorful vegetables. Cubed potatoes and green peas add pops of color and texture, while chickpeas (optional) provide a heartiness often associated with vegetarian options.

Ingredients

  • Rice(1 Kg)
  • Vermicelli Pasta(150g)
  • Cooking Oil(1L approx.)
  • Sultanas)(250g)
  • Roasted Almonds(250g)
  • Chicken Breast(1Kg)
  • Water(3 Cups approx.)
  • Green Peas(250g)
  • Potatoes(1Kg)
  • a Large Onion
  • Handful Chickpeas(2)
  • Biryani/Baharat Powder, to taste
